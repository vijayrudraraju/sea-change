Sea Change: A Musical Simulation

Welcome to the Ocean!

The dynamics & life of the ocean are highly dependent on aspects such as temperature, tides, sea level, and time of day to name a few.

This simulation is meant to evoke a sense of that dynamism by generating an ocean scene and background music with variables polled from an API served by NOAA that reports data from several locations from the West Coast of the US.

INSTRUCTIONS: After loading finishes, you should see a readout of data from Arena Cove, CA in the upper-left corner. To start the simulation, click the button in the upper-right corner that reads "Paused". You can select different locations (and seed the simulation with different data), by using the green dropdown menu.
